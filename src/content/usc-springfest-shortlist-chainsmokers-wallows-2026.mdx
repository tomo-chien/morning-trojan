  p span[style*="font-size"] { line-height: 1.6; }
</style><div style="padding-bottom:12px;padding-left:10px;padding-right:10px;padding-top:12px;"><p style="color:#000000;color:var(--wt-text-on-background-color) !important;font-family:'Helvetica',Arial,sans-serif;font-size:19px;line-height:1.5;text-align:left;"> The committee was close to closing a deal with the Chainsmokers, but the EDM duo had a scheduling conflict, one source said. Wallows, on the other hand, was vetoed by the school’s Department of Public Safety because the indie band apparently has a history of distributing alcohol at concerts. </p></div><style>
  p span[style*="font-size"] { line-height: 1.6; }
</style><div style="padding-bottom:12px;padding-left:10px;padding-right:10px;padding-top:12px;"><p style="color:#000000;color:var(--wt-text-on-background-color) !important;font-family:'Helvetica',Arial,sans-serif;font-size:19px;line-height:1.5;text-align:left;"> “Springfest is a dry event,” DPS Chief Lauretta Hill wrote in a statement. “Wallows was not considered based on this.” </p></div><style>
  p span[style*="font-size"] { line-height: 1.6; }
</style><div style="padding-bottom:12px;padding-left:10px;padding-right:10px;padding-top:12px;"><p style="color:#000000;color:var(--wt-text-on-background-color) !important;font-family:'Helvetica',Arial,sans-serif;font-size:19px;line-height:1.5;text-align:left;"> The Concerts Committee never reached final negotiations with Wallows, one source said, so there’s no guarantee that the group would’ve performed at USC if not for the DPS veto. </p></div><style>
  p span[style*="font-size"] { line-height: 1.6; }
</style><div style="padding-bottom:12px;padding-left:10px;padding-right:10px;padding-top:12px;"><p style="color:#000000;color:var(--wt-text-on-background-color) !important;font-family:'Helvetica',Arial,sans-serif;font-size:19px;line-height:1.5;text-align:left;"> Springfest, which is free for students, is generally one of the most-anticipated events of the school year. This year’s show is <a class="link" href="https://www.instagram.com/p/DVzHJ5fkrQq/?utm_source=ig_web_copy_link&igsh=MzRlODBiNWFlZA==" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" style="-webkit-text-decoration:underline #ac2124;color:#ac2124;text-decoration:underline #ac2124;word-break:break-word;;">set for Saturday</a> in McCarthy Quad and will feature the hyperpop group Frost Children and singer-songwriter Lana Lubany alongside Ty Dolla $ign. </p></div><style>

The Man, Dominic Fike, 2 Chainz, Migos, and Glass Animals. Last year, the rapper Don Toliver headlined the concert, which <a class="link" href="https://www.uscannenbergmedia.com/2025/04/09/springfest-2025-don-toliver-tore-up-the-coliseum/?utm_source=morning-trojan.beehiiv.com&utm_medium=referral&utm_campaign=chainsmokers-wallows-were-on-usc-springfest-shortlist" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" style="-webkit-text-decoration:underline #ac2124;color:#ac2124;text-decoration:underline #ac2124;word-break:break-word;;">was held inside</a> the Coliseum for the first time ever. </p></div><style>

</style><div style="padding-bottom:12px;padding-left:10px;padding-right:10px;padding-top:12px;"><p style="color:#000000;color:var(--wt-text-on-background-color) !important;font-family:'Helvetica',Arial,sans-serif;font-size:19px;line-height:1.5;text-align:left;"> Springfest is a massive undertaking. The events typically host several thousand students in the center of USC’s campus and can quickly go awry without proper planning. In 2017, the Los Angeles Fire Department <a class="link" href="https://www.uscannenbergmedia.com/2017/04/01/usc-springfest-cancelled-due-to-overcrowding/?utm_source=morning-trojan.beehiiv.com&utm_medium=referral&utm_campaign=chainsmokers-wallows-were-on-usc-springfest-shortlist" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" style="-webkit-text-decoration:underline #ac2124;color:#ac2124;text-decoration:underline #ac2124;word-break:break-word;;">shut down the event</a> due to “overcrowding” and transported one person to the hospital. </p></div><style>

</style><div style="padding-bottom:12px;padding-left:10px;padding-right:10px;padding-top:12px;"><p style="color:#000000;color:var(--wt-text-on-background-color) !important;font-family:'Helvetica',Arial,sans-serif;font-size:19px;line-height:1.5;text-align:left;"> Then there’s the matter of wrangling artists. </p></div><style>
